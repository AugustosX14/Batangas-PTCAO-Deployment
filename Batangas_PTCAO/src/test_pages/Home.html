About Batangas

Batangas is a province in the Philippines located in the CALABARZON region in Luzon. Its capital is Batangas City and it is bordered by the provinces of Cavite and Laguna to the north and Quezon to the east.

Known for its beautiful beaches, rich cultural heritage, and diverse tourism offerings, Batangas continues to be one of the Philippines' most visited provinces.
